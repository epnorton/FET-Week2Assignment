More Fun Facts!

  • An estimated 1,864 pandas still roam the wild, while 400 are kept in captivity.
  • In captivity, the average lifespan of a panda is 30 years.
  • Wild pands have a lifespan of 20 years.
  • Pandas don't hibernate.
